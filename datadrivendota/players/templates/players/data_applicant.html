What we are offering

We are offering a one-off 500 match data import by request during our beta.

At the moment, we are rate-limited by Valve's API: we can only import about 70 people per day while keeping up with our other import obligations. We want to keep things fair, so we are accepting data requests via the form below and choosing randomly from the applicant pool if we have more requests than spaces. We will email you when your data is being pulled in.

Gotchas and howto

Please make sure your data is public, or we cannot pull it for you!

(Dota 2 > Settings gear (top left) > Options (middle) > Profile (far right) > Expose Public Data Match.)

To get your steam id, use the XXXX in your steam community URL (ex http://steamcommunity.com/profiles/XXXX), or http://steamidconverter.com/ to get your 64 bit ID from your account name. (You can use the YYYY in http://steamcommunity.com/id/YYYY to get your account name). Make sure this points to your account, not someone else's!
