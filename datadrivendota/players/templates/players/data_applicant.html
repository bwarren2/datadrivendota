What we are offering

During our beta, we are offering a free one-off 200 match data import by request. If there is interest, we will add a premium version: no ads, automatic data import, customizable tracker feed, and more at ~$2/month to help cover costs.

At the moment, we are rate-limited by Valve's API: we can only import about 50 people per day while keeping up with our other import obligations. (We are talking to them about this.) We want to keep things fair, so we are accepting data requests via the form below and choosing randomly from the applicant pool if we have more requests than spaces. We will email you when your data is being pulled in.

Gotchas and howto

Please make sure your data is public, or we cannot pull it for you!

(Dota 2 > Settings gear (top left) > Options (middle) > Profile (far right) > Expose Public Data Match.)

To get your steam id, use the XXXX in your steam community URL (ex http://steamcommunity.com/profiles/XXXX), or open your dota 2 profile in-client and take the number on top of your picture, or http://steamidconverter.com/ to get your 64 bit ID from your account name. (You can use the YYYY in http://steamcommunity.com/id/YYYY to get your account name). Make sure this points to your account, not someone else's!

HINT

These are one-off data pulls. If you want easily to see what happens when you use our tools, you can play a couple of games based on your inferences before you make a data request.
